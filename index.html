Questions Seekers Ask

Is the tarot reading free?

Yes — every reading on The Cat Priestess is completely free, with no account and no payment. Choose a spread and draw.

How do I get a tarot reading?

Hold a question in your heart — or none at all — choose a spread (a single Daily Draw, a three-card Past · Present · Path, or a five-card Inner Journey), and tap Shuffle & Draw. Bastet then reads the cards for you.

Who is Bastet?

Bastet is our oracle guide, inspired by the ancient Egyptian goddess of cats, protection, and the moon. Tap “Ask Bastet” to chat with her any time for warm, intuitive guidance.

Do I need to create an account?

No. The Cat Priestess works instantly in your browser with no sign-up, and can be installed as a free app on your phone.

What do the three tarot spreads mean?

The Daily Draw is one card for today. Past · Present · Path shows where you have been, where you are, and where you are headed. The Inner Journey is a five-card soul map for deeper reflection.
